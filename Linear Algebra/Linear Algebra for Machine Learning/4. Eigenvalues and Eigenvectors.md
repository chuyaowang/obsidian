## Eigenvectors

- $Ax=\lambda x$ 
- Mostly n eigenvectors for a n by n matrix
- $x$ is also an eigenvector of $A^n$: $A^nx=\lambda^nx$
- When A is not [singular](Singular%20Matrix.md), it is non-invertible, $\lambda$ is not 0, and $A^{-1}x=\frac{1}{\lambda}x$
- $e^{At}x=e^{\lambda t}x$
